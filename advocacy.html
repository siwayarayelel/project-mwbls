What makes Filipino made-products unique?

Filipinos made carvings are solely made by hand. The lack of influence on the art of Philippines wood carving by industrialization, mechanization, as well as modern technology systems is what truly makes the art unique. Essentially, what makes Philippines wood carving so incredible is the fact that the natives have managed to preserve the traditions of ancient crafts.

The rise of Bamboo as replacement for woodcarving in the Philippines

Bamboo is becoming a widely-used material in some regions of the Philippines, contributing to the local economy due to the high-interest visitors of the country show when it comes to bamboo-made carvings. In fact, the Philippines is the 5th largest bamboo exporter in the entire world, based on recent research from 2018.
